Humidity

Humidity does not show a strong correlation to latitude. The visualization shows a great variety of humidity percentages at different latitudes. Therefore, it cannot be concluded that there is a conclusive pattern between humidity and latitude. Editor Note: there were two rogue data points (nearly 300%) that skewed the graph.
